Personalized News Recommendation with Late Fusion

Investigated Late Fusion strategies combining symbolic SF-IDF+ and Sentence-BERT for news recommendation. Experiments on MIND-small demonstrated that hybrid fusion underperformed a simple BERT-only baseline. Identified key issues including entity-level noise from WordNet expansion and misalignment between sparse and dense score distributions. The study concludes that for entity-dense headlines, precision-centric dense encoders are often superior to elaborate hybrid schemes.
